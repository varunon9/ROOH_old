Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the vision of ROOH?

Our main aim is to eradicate poverty and illiteracy from India and make India a better place to live. Our vision is to eradicate all social evils from our country and help the needy people in every way possible.

2. How old is ROOH?

ROOH was founded on April 22, 2011 by Mr. Abhishek Chauhan.

3. Who are the people behind ROOH?

We are students of NIT Kurukshetra ,Kurukshetra ,Haryana.Visit Members

4. What exactly you do?

We select poor children based on their academic performance,family status and extra curriculum activities and provide them admission in NIVEDITA PUBLIC SCHOOL. We work for their overall development.

We also organise Blood Donation Camp jointly with Red Cross Society twice a year.Visit Our programmes

5. Why do you stress mainly on education?

Education alone has the power to liberate people from the vicious cycle of poverty - not only in terms of earning a livelihood but also in becoming aware of the rights and freedoms that a citizen is endowed with. Thus empowering individuals and making them self-sufficient.

6. How can one associate with ROOH?

You can become a member/supporter/volunteer.Contact us

7. How can I contribute to ROOH?

You can contribute to us by donating some amount or by joining us directly or indirectly. Kindly Contact us
